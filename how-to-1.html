1. Understanding the basics of Beekeeping:

  • Types of Bees:

    • In a hive, there are three main types of bees - the queen, worker bees (females), and drones (males).

  • Life Cycles:

    • Bees go through a complete metamorphosis, starting as eggs, progressing to larvae, then pupae, and finally emerging as adults.

2.Selecting Equipments

  • Hives:

    • The most common hive design is the Langstroth hive, which consists of stacked boxes with removable frames.

  • Frames:

    • Frames hold the honeycomb and brood comb. They are removable for inspection and harvesting.

  • Protective Gear:

    • Wear a bee suit, gloves, and a veil to protect yourself from stings.

  • Smoker:

    • A smoker is used to calm the bees during hive inspections.
